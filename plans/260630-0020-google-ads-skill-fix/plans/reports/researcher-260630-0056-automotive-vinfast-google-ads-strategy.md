## 2. EV Marketing: What's Different from ICE

### Consumer Concerns Driving Messaging

**Primary EV Concerns (2024 research):**
1. **Range Anxiety** - Battery life limitations
2. **Charge Anxiety** (new shift) - Charging station access/reliability
3. **High Initial Cost** - Battery capacity pricing
4. **Technology Trust** - Long-term durability concerns

### EV-Specific Messaging Strategies

**Address Range Anxiety:**
- **Convenience focus:** "Home charging = full tank every morning"
- **Infrastructure confidence:** "X charging stations in Vietnam network"
- **Real-world demonstrations:** "Real range: 280km city driving"
- **Technology reassurance:** "CATL Shenxing: 0-70% charge in 5 minutes"

**Avoid Generic Claims:**
- ❌ "Longest range in class" ( unverifiable, policy violation)
- ✅ "280km WLTP city range" (specific, verifiable)
- ❌ "Best charging network" (absolute claim)
- ✅ "500+ charging stations nationwide" (verifiable stat)

### ICE vs. EV Conversion Considerations

**ICE (Internal Combustion Engine):**
- Shorter consideration cycle (days-weeks)
- Familiar technology, less education needed
- Fuel economy messaging
- Maintenance cost comparisons

**EV:**
- Longer consideration cycle (weeks-months)
- Requires education + reassurance
- Total cost of ownership (TCO) messaging
- Battery warranty/lease programs
- Charging infrastructure education

**Key Insight:** EV ads need **more touchpoints, longer nurturing, higher trust-building** than ICE equivalents.

## 4. Budget Realism: Tier Recommendations

### Honest Assessment of $500/Month

**$500/month = ~$16.67/day = ~6-7 clicks/day @ $2.41 CPC**

**Reality Check:**
- **6-7 clicks/day** = insufficient data for optimization
- **186 clicks/month** = ~14 leads/month @ 7.76% CVR
- **CPL $35.71** = within $38.86 benchmark (but tiny sample)
- **No smart bidding** = need 30 conversions (takes 2+ months at this rate)

**Verdict:** $500/month is **minimum viable testing budget only**. Not enough for meaningful lead generation or optimization.

#### **Tier 1: Minimum Viable ($1,500-3,000/month)**

**Best For:** Single city (HCM or Hanoi), single model focus (e.g., VF3 only)

- 1 campaign, 2-3 ad groups (branded + non-branded + competitor)
- Geo: Single city (30km radius)

#### **Tier 2: Recommended ($3,000-8,000/month)**

**Best For:** National Vietnam coverage, multi-model campaign

## 9. Conversion Tracking Setup

### Primary Conversion: Test-Drive Booking

**Setup Requirements:**
1. **Conversion Action:** Create in Google Ads (Conversions → New Conversion Action)
2. **Tracking Method:** Google Tag Manager + HTML form submission
3. **Value:** Assign value (e.g., $100 = estimated lead value)
4. **Counting:** "One" per click (unique bookings)

**Tracking Code Implementation:**
```html
<!-- On booking confirmation page -->
<script>
  gtag('event', 'conversion', {
    'send_to': 'AW-CONVERSION_ID/CONVERSION_LABEL',
    'value': 100.0,
    'currency': 'USD'
  });
</script>
```

**Landing Page Flow:**
1. **Landing Page:** Model-specific (e.g., /vf3-landing)
2. **Form Fields:** Name, Phone, Preferred Date, Dealer Location
3. **Submission:** Redirect to /thank-you with conversion tracking
4. **CRM Push:** Send lead to dealer CRM for follow-up

### Secondary Conversions (Track All)

| Conversion | Value | Counting | Priority |
|------------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Test-Drive Booking** | $100 | One | Primary (optimize for this) |
| **Brochure Download** | $10 | One | Secondary |
| **Dealer Inquiry (Form)** | $50 | One | Secondary |
| **Phone Call (>=2min)** | $75 | One | Secondary |
| **Dealer Visit (Offline)** | $200 | One | Tertiary (import offline) |

### Offline Conversion Import (Long-Term)

**Why Important:**
- Google optimizes for last-click (test-drive booking)
- But business goal is vehicle **sale** (30-90 days later)
- Offline import connects test-drive → sale pipeline

**Setup Process:**
1. **Export Data:** From dealer CRM (Customer ID + Test-Drive Date + Purchase Date + Revenue)
2. **Upload to Google:** Offline Conversions tool
3. **Lag Period:** 30-90 days (adjust for industry)
4. **Use for:** Bidding optimization (Smart Bidding learns true value)

**Data Format (CSV):**
```csv
Google Click ID,Conversion Name,Conversion Time,Conversion Value
GOAAB1E2K3L4M5N6P7Q8R9S0,Test_Drive_to_Sale,2024-03-15 08:00:00,25000
```

**Impact:** Allows tCPA/tROAS to optimize for **sales**, not just test-drives.
