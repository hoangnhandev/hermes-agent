# Automotive Audience + Retargeting Strategy

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-30
**Focus:** In-market audiences, retargeting playbook, life events, remarketing

## Cold Audiences (Prospecting)

### 1. In-Market Auto Intenders

**Google Predefined Audiences:**

**Auto Intenders (Broad):**
- **Definition:** Actively researching or shopping for vehicles
- **Size:** Large audience (millions in Vietnam)
- **Timing:** Last 30-90 days of auto-related activity
- **Best For:** Campaign launch, awareness phase

**SUV Intenders (Segment-Specific):**
- **Definition:** Researching SUVs specifically
- **Relevance:** VF5, VF6, VF7, VF8, VF9 campaigns
- **Message:** "So sánh SUV điện với SUV xăng. Lái thử hôm nay."

**EV Buyers (High Intent):**
- **Definition:** Researching electric vehicles specifically
- **Size:** Smaller but high-intent audience
- **Relevance:** All Vinfast campaigns (EV-only brand)
- **Message:** "So sánh VinFast với Tesla, Hyundai. Xem lợi thế."

**Campaign Strategy:**
- **Bid Adjustment:** +20-30% on in-market audiences
- **Budget:** 20-25% of prospecting budget
- **Creative:** Emphasize comparison, competitive advantages

### 2. Custom Intent Audiences (EV Researchers)

**Definition:** Users researching EV-related topics on YouTube/Search

**Keywords/URLs to Target:**
- "xe điện", "electric car Vietnam", "EV charging"
- "Tesla Model Y review", "Hyundai Ioniq 5 comparison"
- "how to charge EV at home", "EV charging stations"
- YouTube channels: EV review sites, automotive reviewers

**Best For:** conquest campaigns, educational messaging

**Message:** "Still researching? See why 10,000+ Vietnamese chose VinFast EVs."

**Campaign Strategy:**
- **Bid Adjustment:** +15-25% on custom intent
- **Budget:** 15-20% of prospecting budget
- **Creative:** Educational, comparison-focused

### 3. Demographic + Affinity Audiences

**Age Targeting:**
- **Primary:** 28-55 years old (prime car-buying age)
- **Young Professionals:** 25-35 (VF3, VF6 targeting)
- **Families:** 30-45 (VF5, VF7, VF9 targeting)
- **Luxury Buyers:** 40-60 (VF8, VF9 targeting)

**Income Targeting (Inferred):**
- **High Income:** Likely to afford VF8-9 (650M-850M VND)
- **Middle Income:** VF3-5 target (240M-450M VND)
- **Budget Conscious:** VF3, battery lease messaging

**Affinity Categories:**
- **Tech Enthusiasts:** Early adopters, tech-savvy
- **Green Living:** Sustainability-focused, EV-conscious
- **Auto Enthusiasts:** Car hobbyists, performance-focused
- **Family-Centric:** Vehicle safety, space priority

**Campaign Strategy:**
- **Bid Adjustment:** +10-20% on affinity audiences
- **Budget:** 15% of prospecting budget
- **Creative:** Tailored to audience interests (tech, family, green)

### 4. Life Event Targeting

**New Parents (VF5-7 Family SUVs):**
- **Trigger:** Recently had child (last 6-12 months)
- **Concern:** Child safety, air quality (EV advantage)
- **Message:** "An toàn cho gia đình, không khí sạch trong xe."
- **Budget Allocation:** 10% of family SUV campaigns

**Moving to Suburbs (VF8-9 Commuter Range):**
- **Trigger:** Recent home purchase (suburban areas)
- **Concern:** Commuter range, charging infrastructure
- **Message:** "280km cho mỗi lần sạc. Đi làm hàng ngày, không lo hết pin."
- **Budget Allocation:** 10% of premium SUV campaigns

**Recent Graduates (VF3 First Car):**
- **Trigger:** Graduated university (last 12-24 months)
- **Concern:** Affordability, style, technology
- **Message:** "Xe điện đầu tiên, giá từ 240 triệu. Công nghệ vượt trội."
- **Budget Allocation:** 10% of VF3 campaign

**Marriage/Engagement (VF7-9 Family Planning):**
- **Trigger:** Recently married or engaged
- **Concern:** Future family planning, vehicle space
- **Message:** "Chuẩn bị cho tương lai. SUV điện 7 chỗ, không khí sạch."
- **Budget Allocation:** 15% of premium campaigns

## Retargeting Audiences (Nurturing)

### Audience Definition Rules

**Website Visitors (No Conversion):**
- **Membership Duration:** 30 days (exclude >30 days)
- **Pages Viewed:** Any model page (VF3-9)
- **Exclusions:** Test-drive booked, brochure downloaded, dealer inquiry submitted
- **Goal:** Nurture to test-drive booking

**YouTube Video Viewers:**
- **Membership Duration:** 14 days (video decays faster)
- **Engagement:** Watched 50%+ of video
- **Video Types:** Model reviews, test drives, technology showcases
- **Goal:** Drive to test-drive booking

**Price/Brochure Page Visitors:**
- **Membership Duration:** 30 days
- **Pages Viewed:** Pricing page, brochure download page
- **Exclusions:** Test-drive booked
- **Goal:** Convert research to booking

**Shopping Cart Abandoners:**
- **Membership Duration:** 7 days (hot lead)
- **Action:** Started test-drive booking, didn't submit
- **Goal:** Complete booking process

### 1. Website Visitors Remarketing

**Audience Definition:**
```
Visited VinFast website in last 30 days
AND did NOT complete test-drive booking
AND did NOT download brochure
```

**Campaign Structure:**
- **Campaign Type:** Responsive Search Ads (Search Network)
- **Budget:** 15% of total budget
- **Bid Strategy:** tCPA (target $40-50, higher than cold)
- **Frequency Cap:** 3 impressions/user/day

**Creative Rotation (Weekly):**

**Week 1: Range Reassurance**
```
Headline: 280km Quãng Đường cho 1 Lần Sạc
Description: Đi làm hàng ngày (40km), sạc hàng tuần. Đừng lo về phạm vi.
```

**Week 2: Charging Convenience**
```
Headline: Sạc Tại Nhà Hoặc 500 Trạm Sạc
Description: Đầy pin mỗi sáng. Sạc nhanh 0-70% trong 5 phút.
```

**Week 3: Cost Savings**
```
Headline: Tiết Kiệm 50% Chi Phế Nhiên Liệu
Description: So sánh với xe xăng. TCO calculator ngay.
```

**Week 4: Warranty Confidence**
```
Headline: 10 Năm Bảo Hành - Không Lo Hại Pin
Description: Thuê pin linh hoạt. Đổi pin miễn phí nếu lỗi.
```

**Landing Page:** Same model pages they visited, with prominent "Book Test Drive" CTA

### 2. YouTube Video Viewers Remarketing

**Audience Definition:**
```
Watched 50%+ of VinFast video
Within last 14 days
Video types: Model reviews, test drives, tech showcases
```

**Campaign Structure:**
- **Campaign Type:** Video Ads (YouTube) + Responsive Display Ads
- **Budget:** 10% of total budget
- **Bid Strategy:** Maximize Clicks (brand awareness + site visit)
- **Frequency Cap:** 2 impressions/user/day

**Creative Strategy:**

**Video Ad (15 seconds):**
- **Hook:** "Liked what you saw in the video?"
- **Body:** "Experience VF8's acceleration yourself. Free test drive."
- **CTA:** "Book Test Drive Today"

**Display Ad (Gmail + Display Network):**
- **Image:** VF8/other model they watched
- **Message:** "See VF8 in person. Book free test drive."
- **CTA:** "Schedule Now"

**Landing Page:** Model-specific page + booking form

### 3. Price/Brochure Page Visitors

**Audience Definition:**
```
Visited pricing page or brochure download page
Last 30 days
Did NOT complete test-drive booking
```

**Campaign Structure:**
- **Campaign Type:** Responsive Search Ads
- **Budget:** 8% of total budget
- **Bid Strategy:** tCPA (target $45-55, lower intent than cart abandoners)
- **Frequency Cap:** 2 impressions/user/week (lower urgency)

**Creative Strategy:**

**Ad Copy:**
```
Headline: Price Transparent - Compare Today
Description: Compare VF3 to gas cars. See 5-year savings. Get brochure.
```

**Landing Page:** Model comparison page + TCO calculator + brochure download

### 4. Shopping Cart Abandoners (Hot Lead)

**Audience Definition:**
```
Started test-drive booking form
Did NOT complete submission
Last 7 days (hot lead, short window)
```

**Campaign Structure:**
- **Campaign Type:** Responsive Search Ads
- **Budget:** 5% of total budget (small but high-value)
- **Bid Strategy:** Manual CPC with +30% bid increase (urgent)
- **Frequency Cap:** 1 impression/user/day (avoid annoyance)

**Creative Strategy:**

**Ad Copy (Urgency + Assistance):**
```
Headline: Complete Your Booking - Free Test Drive
Description: Almost done! Finish booking in 30 seconds. No obligation.
```

**Landing Page:** Pre-filled booking form (remember their details)

### 5. Similar Audiences (Lookalikes)

**Source Audience:** Test-drive bookers (after 50+ conversions)

**Similar Audience Parameters:**
- **Source:** Users who completed test-drive booking
- **Location:** Vietnam (same geography)
- **Size:** 2-5% of Vietnam population (~1-2.5M users)
- **Stability:** Wait 14 days after source audience reaches 100+

**Campaign Structure:**
- **Campaign Type:** Responsive Search Ads + Performance Max
- **Budget:** 12% of total budget
- **Bid Strategy:** tCPA (target $50-60, cold lookalike audience)

**Creative Strategy:**
```
Headline: Customers Like You Chose VinFast
Description: See why 10,000+ Vietnamese booked test drives. Experience it yourself.
```

**Landing Page:** Model lineup page + booking form

## Remarketing Frequency Caps

### Frequency Limits (Per User)

| Campaign Type | Daily Impression Limit | Weekly Impression Limit |
|---------------|------------------------|-------------------------|
| **Website Visitors** | 3/day | 10/week |
| **YouTube Viewers** | 2/day | 5/week |
| **Price/Brochure Visitors** | 2/day | 4/week |
| **Cart Abandoners** | 1/day | 2/week |
| **Similar Audiences** | 3/day | 10/week |

### Creative Rotation Rules

**Same Audience, Different Messages:**
1. **Week 1:** Range reassurance (280km)
2. **Week 2:** Charging convenience (home + public)
3. **Week 3:** Cost savings (TCO calculator)
4. **Week 4:** Warranty confidence (10-year)
5. **Repeat cycle** with variations

**Burnout Prevention:**
- **Exclude converters:** Users who booked test-drive → exclude 90 days
- **Exclude recent buyers:** Users who purchased → exclude 180 days
- **Frequency monitoring:** If CTR drops below 2%, reduce frequency

## Audience Exclusions (Critical)

### Conversion Exclusions

**Exclude These Users from Prospecting:**
- Test-drive bookers (90 days)
- Brochure downloaders (30 days)
- Vehicle purchasers (180 days)
- Dealer service customers (90 days)

**Why:** Don't pay for users who already converted. Focus on new prospects.

### Competitor Exclusions

**Optional (If Not Conquesting):**
- Tesla website visitors
- Hyundai EV researchers
- Wuling EV shoppers

**Why:** Avoid bidding on users actively shopping competitors (unless conquest campaign)

### Non-Relevant Exclusions

**Exclude from Remarketing:**
- Employees (IP-based exclusion)
- Agencies/contractors (IP-based exclusion)
- Bot traffic (exclude known bot IPs)

## Audience Performance Monitoring

### Weekly Metrics to Track

**Audience-Level Metrics:**
- CTR by audience (in-market vs custom intent vs life event)
- CPC by audience (bid adjustment ROI)
- Conversion rate by audience (which audiences convert best)
- Cost per conversion by audience (efficiency ranking)

**Remarketing Metrics:**
- Audience size (is remarketing pool growing?)
- Frequency (are users seeing too many ads?)
- CTR decay (drop below 2% = burnout)
- Conversion rate (which message works best?)

### Monthly Audience Optimization

**Scale Winners:**
- Top-converting audiences → increase budget by 20-30%
- High CTR audiences → create dedicated ad groups
- Low CPA audiences → increase bid adjustments (+20-30%)

**Pause Losers:**
- Low CTR (<2%) audiences → pause or rotate creative
- High CPA (>2x benchmark) audiences → exclude or reduce bids
- Small audiences (<1,000 users) → combine with similar audiences

**Test New Audiences:**
- New Google pre-defined audiences (auto launches new audiences quarterly)
- New life events (Google adds new events periodically)
- Custom combinations (in-market + affinity)

## Vietnam-Specific Audience Nuances

### Mobile-First Audiences

**Device Behavior:**
- 70-80% of Vietnam traffic is mobile
- Mobile audiences have shorter sessions (research on-the-go)
- Desktop audiences have higher conversion intent (serious buyers)

**Strategy:**
- **Mobile audiences:** Focus on awareness + consideration
- **Desktop audiences:** Focus on test-drive booking + purchase

### Urban vs. Rural Audiences

**Urban (HCM, Hanoi, Da Nang):**
- Higher income → target VF7-9
- Better charging infrastructure → emphasize convenience
- Traffic congestion → emphasize EV benefits (no fuel stops)

**Rural (Provinces):**
- Lower income → target VF3-5
- Limited charging → emphasize home charging + battery lease
- Longer distances → emphasize range

### Family vs. Individual Buyers

**Family Buyers (VF5-9):**
- Concern: Safety, space, air quality
- Audience: Parents 30-45, married
- Message: "An toàn cho gia đình, không khí sạch"

**Individual Buyers (VF3-6):**
- Concern: Style, technology, affordability
- Audience: Singles 25-35, young professionals
- Message: "Công nghệ vượt trội, thiết kế sành điệu"
