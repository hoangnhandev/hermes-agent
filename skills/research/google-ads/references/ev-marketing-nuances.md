# EV Marketing: What's Different from ICE

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-30
**Focus:** Range anxiety, charging infrastructure, consideration cycle, messaging strategies

## Core Consumer Concerns (2024 Research)

### Primary EV Anxiety Factors

1. **Range Anxiety** (Traditional)
   - Battery life limitations
   - Fear of running out of power
   - Uncertainty about real-world range

2. **Charge Anxiety** (New Shift 2024)
   - Charging station access/reliability
   - Charging speed and convenience
   - Infrastructure availability

3. **High Initial Cost**
   - Battery capacity pricing
   - Total cost of ownership questions
   - Resale value uncertainty

4. **Technology Trust**
   - Battery longevity concerns
   - Long-term durability
   - Manufacturer reliability

## EV vs. ICE Marketing Differences

### Consideration Cycle Length

| Vehicle Type | Consideration Cycle | Touchpoints Needed | Trust Building |
|-------------|---------------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **ICE (Gas/Diesel)** | Days-Weeks | 3-5 | Familiar technology |
| **EV** | Weeks-Months | 7-12 | Requires education + proof |

**Implication:** EV campaigns need **2-3x more touchpoints** and **longer nurturing** than ICE equivalents.

### Message Focus Comparison

**ICE Messages:**
- Fuel efficiency (km/liter, mpg)
- Maintenance cost savings
- Performance (horsepower, acceleration)
- Interior features, comfort

**EV Messages:**
- Range per charge (real-world, not just WLTP)
- Charging convenience (home, public, fast charging)
- Total cost of ownership (TCO: fuel + maintenance savings)
- Battery warranty/lease programs
- Charging infrastructure availability

## Range Anxiety Messaging Strategies

### DO: Reassure With Specifics

✅ **"280km WLTP city range"** - Specific, verifiable
✅ **"Real-world range: 250km urban driving"** - Honest, transparent
✅ **"Full charge every morning at home"** - Convenience emphasis
✅ **"500+ charging stations nationwide"** - Infrastructure confidence
✅ **"0-70% charge in 5 minutes (CATL tech)"** - Technology reassurance

### DON'T: Make Absolute Claims

❌ **"Longest range in class"** - Unverifiable, Google Ads policy violation
❌ **"Best charging network"** - Absolute claim, requires proof
❌ **"Never run out of power"** - Unrealistic promise
❌ **"Unlimited range"** - Impossible claim

- Electricity cheaper than gasoline/diesel
- Example: "Tiết kiệm 50% chi phí nhiên liệu"

**Maintenance Savings:**
- No oil changes, fewer moving parts
- Example: "Không thay dầu, ít bảo dưỡng hơn xe xăng"

**Incentives/Rebates (Vietnam):**

## EV-Specific Policy Compliance

### Google Ads Policy: Environmental Claims

**Allowed (if true and verifiable):**
- "Zero tailpipe emissions" (technically true for EVs)
- "No direct emissions" (accurate wording)
- "Lower emissions than gas cars" (comparative, can be proven)

**Prohibited (absolute claims):**
- "100% clean energy" (unless entire supply chain is renewable)
- "Zero emissions vehicle" (false if grid uses fossil fuels)
- "Completely carbon-neutral" (lifecycle emissions exist)

### Price + Value Claims

**Allowed:**
- "Lower total cost of ownership" (if TCO calculation provided)
- "Cheaper to run than gas" (if fuel comparison data provided)

**Prohibited:**
- "Pays for itself in X years" (unrealistic guarantee)
- "Free fuel forever" (electricity costs money)
- "Never pay for gas again" (true but misleading: pay for electricity)

## EV Common Objections + Counters

| Objection | Counter-Messaging |
|-----------|-------------------|
| "Range anxiety" | "280km real-world range. Daily commute: 40km. Charge weekly, not daily." |
| "Charging is slow" | "0-70% in 5 minutes (fast charging) or overnight at home (convenience)" |
| "EVs are expensive" | "Lower TCO: save 50% on fuel, no oil changes. 5-year breakeven." |
| "Battery replacement cost" | "10-year warranty. Battery lease option (no replacement cost)." |
| "Not enough charging stations" | "500+ stations nationwide. Home charging = full tank every morning." |
| "Grid can't handle EVs" | "Smart charging (off-peak). Vietnam investing in renewable energy." |
